## Personas

### Persona: Product Owner
- Role: Oversees delivery priorities and business value.
- Goals: Track progress, resolve blockers, and confirm that work is moving forward.
- Needs: Visibility into workload, overdue items, and task health.
- Constraints: Requires summary views and clear reporting.

### Persona: Team Lead
- Role: Coordinates team execution and membership.
- Goals: Organize work, assign effort, and maintain team productivity.
- Needs: Team-level task visibility, workload balance, and reporting.
- Constraints: Must manage tasks within authorized team boundaries.

### Persona: Team Member
- Role: Executes assigned work and collaborates with others.
- Goals: Complete tasks, update progress, and communicate status.
- Needs: Clear task details, comments, updates, and notifications.
- Constraints: Access is limited to allowed tasks and teams.

### Persona: Administrator
- Role: Governs users, roles, teams, and system settings.
- Goals: Maintain platform integrity and support adoption.
- Needs: Broad management controls and audit visibility.
- Constraints: Must preserve security, governance, and compliance controls.
